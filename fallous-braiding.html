Our Story

Rooted in culture,
refined by craft.

Fallou's African Hair Braiding is a proudly Black-owned salon that has served Oak Park and Metro Detroit for over two decades. We bring generations of West African braiding knowledge to every client who walks through our doors.

Every style we create is a bridge between tradition and the present — a living expression of beauty practices that have empowered communities for centuries. Our stylists bring patience, skill, and genuine care to every appointment.
